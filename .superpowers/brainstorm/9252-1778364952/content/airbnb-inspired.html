SoundSearch — Airbnb-Inspired Design

Dark theme adaptation of Airbnb's design principles

Design Philosophy

  • Photo-first cards: Album art dominates, text is secondary
  • Modest typography: Weights 400-600, not 700+. Let imagery carry visual weight
  • Soft shapes: 12-14px card radius, pill-shaped search/buttons, circular icons
  • Single accent: Teal (#0d9488) for primary CTAs only — 90% dark + white text
  • One shadow tier: Subtle elevation on hover, flat otherwise
  • Generous spacing: 64px section bands, 16px card gutters
